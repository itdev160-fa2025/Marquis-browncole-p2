Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew

Needed ingredients

  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon canned pumpkin
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice (or more for garnish)
  • 1 cup cold brew coffee
  • Ice cubes

Directions

  1. Combine heavy whipping cream, milk, canned pumpkin, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and pumpkin pie spice in a small jar. Froth with a milk frother until foamy.
  2. Place ice cubes in a large glass. Pour the cold brew over the ice and top with the cold foam.
  3. Sprinkle the cold foam with additional pumpkin pie spice and serve immediately.

Nutrition

1 serving: Approximately 140 calories, 8g fat, 5g saturated fat, 10mg cholesterol, 40mg sodium, 15g carbohydrate, 10g sugars, 2g protein.
